FS Business Forum 

The FS Business Forum (officially Frankfurt School Business Forum) is an annual, student-run, two-day business conference held at the Frankfurt School of Finance & Management in Frankfurt am Main, Germany. Founded in 2019, the conference attracts over 600 participants and brings together students from international universities with leaders from business, finance and politics. 

The FS Business Forum is organized as an official, non-profit student initiative of Frankfurt School and is regarded as the flagship event* of the institution. It is described as one of the largest student-led business congresses in Europe and is known for its international speaker lineup. 

Shape 

Purpose and Concept 

The primary goal of the FS Business Forum is to encourage direct dialogue between students and industry leaders. In contrast to traditional career fairs, the Forum emphasizes personal interaction, open discussion, and long-term networking opportunities. 

The conference aims to connect students from leading European and international universities with professionals from their respective industries while fostering debate on current economic, political, and societal challenges. 

Shape 

Conference Format 

The FS Business Forum features a broad range of interactive formats, including keynote speeches, panel discussions, Q&A sessions, workshops, interviews, podcasts, and meet-and-greet formats. Career-oriented workshops and recruiting-focused networking sessions form an integral part of the event. 

In addition to formal conference programming, the Forum includes social and networking formats such as a Corporate Networking Dinner and a Wine Tasting, designed to facilitate informal exchange between participants and speakers. 

Each edition is organized around a central theme. The most recent edition was held under the theme “A Resilient Europe – Empowering the Future”, reflecting the contemporary economic and geopolitical challenges facing Europe.

Shape 

Career and Recruiting Activities 

Recruiting and career development are embedded throughout the conference primarily via workshops and structured networking formats rather than a standalone career fair. 

Participating companies offer workshops, interviews, and speed-dating sessions with students. Recruiting partners have included firms such as Berenberg, Roland Berger, BlackRock, Bankhaus Metzler and ODDO BHF among others. 

Shape 

Entrepreneurship and Innovation 

The FS Business Forum includes a Pitch Battle, providing start-ups with the opportunity to present their business ideas to an audience and jury. The format reflects the Forum’s expansion toward entrepreneurship and innovation-focused content alongside its traditional business and finance orientation.

Shape 

Organization 

A distinguishing feature of the FS Business Forum is that the entire conference is planned and executed by approximately 40 students of Frankfurt School of Finance & Management.  

The organizing team is structured into several departments, including: 

Corporate Relations 

Speaker Relations 

Marketing 

IT 

Event Management 

The students are supported by Frankfurt School Alumni e.V., the Frankfurt School Student Council, the university itself, and the corporate partners of the respective conference. 

Shape 

Institutional Affiliation 

The FS Business Forum is officially recognized as a student initiative of Frankfurt School of Finance & Management. The university provides logistical support and academic endorsement, including recognition of student involvement in official extracurricular transcripts. 

Shape 

History 

The FS Business Forum was founded in 2019 with the objective of creating a more personal and direct connection between students and industry leaders than traditional recruiting events typically allow. Since its inception, the Forum has expanded its scope to include additional formats such as corporate dinners, wine tastings, and entrepreneurship-focused events.

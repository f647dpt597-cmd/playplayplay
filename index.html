About the Forum

One of Europe's Largest Student-Run Conferences

The FS Business Forum is an annual, student-run, two-day business conference held at the Frankfurt School of Finance & Management. Founded in 2019, we bring together over 700 participants from international universities with leaders from business, finance, and politics.

As the flagship event of Frankfurt School, we are recognized as one of the largest student-led business congresses in Europe. Our mission is to encourage direct dialogue between students and industry leaders through personal interaction, open discussion, and meaningful networking.
